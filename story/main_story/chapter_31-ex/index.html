Story Summary

Project Stigma has it's early phases activated giving Kevin the powers of Finality that Prometheus was holding onto, which pulled Teri who was near the moon into a dream which she uses her powers to consume and access the Kaslana Stigma space. Here Teri gets a lore drop from the WIll of the Stigma before running into Kevin. Kevin meshes his power with that of Shamash and Finality and enters a new version of his Mantis form. Kevin defeats the Will of the Stigma in a battle then defeats Teri and putting her in a place where she can hibernate until the new world is created.
